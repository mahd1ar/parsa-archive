+medical tourism

parsa healthcare

parsa healthcare is a medical tourism facilitator based in Iran offering high-quality and affordable healthcare and travel services. In line with its mission to provide medical and cosmetic treatments to foreign people, parsa healthcare collaborates with the most prestigious hospitals, clinics, specialists and doctors in Iran.


parsa healthcare provides patients with other travel services such as visa, airport pick-up (and drop-off), private transfer, booking accommodations, flights, round-the-clock interpreting and tour guiding services, nursing, sightseeing tours, and so on.

  • why health tourism?

    In recent decades, many people prefer to travelling abroad to obtain medical treatment because of:

    1. lack of modern equipped hospitals and skilled surgeons
    2. long waiting list for certain procedures
    3. high cost of healthcare at country.

  • why iran?

    Iran is ideal destination for health tourism.
    Medical services in Iran is cheaper than the world while the Healthcare establishments are well equipped and professional
